Now is part of the moment. The moment when you got fed up with business as usual. When you decided that what worked before wouldn't work anymore. When you were so determined to change that you believed you already had.

But change isn't a decision. It's a million tiny choices; each individually inconsequential, giving shape to a greater whole. The moment—this whole—defines you. There is no shortcut, no back door, no epiphany that can free you from the outcome of your choices.

The ladder from hell is hot to the touch. The price of real change is high; it accepts nothing short of complete fidelity. Pain is part of the moment. Loneliness, frustration, failure, and regret; you will stumble through emotional pain like you've never imagined. And every step upward makes the fall to rock bottom further. This moment will break you.

But eventually you look back and realize that you are where you wanted to be. You appreciate how entirely you are the sum of your choices. And at the head of this journey of self, before turning to the path ahead, you pause. Pride is part of the moment, too.
